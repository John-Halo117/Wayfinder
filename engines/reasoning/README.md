# Reasoning

## Purpose

Evaluates interpretations and evidence to infer conclusions under uncertainty.

## Owns

Inference, argument structure, contradiction handling, confidence posture, and reasoned conclusions.

## Does Not Own

Observation storage, view presentation, commitments, route choice, or action execution.

## Inputs

Evidence, interpretations, objectives, constraints, policies, and durable knowledge.

## Outputs

Reasoned conclusions, uncertainty assessments, open questions, and proof inputs.

## Dependencies

ARK, Interpretation, Evidence Contracts, Policy, Objectives.

## Consumers

Views, Jarvis, MICE, VALOR, Foundry, and domains.
