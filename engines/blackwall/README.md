# Blackwall

## Purpose

Owns protection boundary behavior.

## Owns

Threat posture, protection decisions, boundary hardening recommendations, and protection constraints.

## Does Not Own

Permission vocabulary, cryptography service behavior, operations, or policy ownership.

## Inputs

Assets, policies, permissions, value/risk assessments, network findings, and context.

## Outputs

Protection findings, protection constraints, and boundary recommendations.

## Dependencies

Policy, Permission Contracts, VALOR, NetWatch, Identity.

## Consumers

Operations, Jarvis, domains, internal applications, and operators.
