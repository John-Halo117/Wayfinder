# Interpretation

## Purpose

Produces meaning candidates from observations, representations, and relationships.

## Owns

Interpretive hypotheses, semantic candidates, ambiguity surfaces, and candidate meanings.

## Does Not Own

Reality preservation, final inference, navigation, commitments, or views.

## Inputs

Observations, representations, relationship topology, evidence, context, and schemas.

## Outputs

Interpretation candidates with uncertainty and evidence references.

## Dependencies

ARK, WEAVE, Evidence Contracts, Views.

## Consumers

Reasoning, Views, Jarvis, and domains.
