# WEAVE

## Purpose

Maintains relationship topology among durable entities, observations, assets, and concepts.

## Owns

Relationship topology, adjacency, relationship context, and connection structure.

## Does Not Own

Reality preservation, interpretation, reasoning, navigation decisions, or storage infrastructure.

## Inputs

Durable reality, identity references, evidence references, asset references, and context.

## Outputs

Relationship topology and relationship surfaces for interpretation, reasoning, views, and navigation.

## Dependencies

ARK, Identity Contracts, Evidence Contracts, Asset Contracts, Provenance Contracts.

## Consumers

Interpretation, Reasoning, Views, Jarvis, ZWLib, and domains.
