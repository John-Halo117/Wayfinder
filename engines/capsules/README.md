# Capsules

## Purpose

Preserves continuity packages for future re-entry.

## Owns

Capsules, re-entry context, continuity bundles, capsule maturity, handoff boundaries.

## Does Not Own

Generic storage, reality preservation, navigation, or commitment ownership.

## Inputs

Durable knowledge, context references, state references, evidence references, objectives, and owner references.

## Outputs

Continuity capsules, re-entry context, handoff packages, and capsule maturity signals.

## Dependencies

ARK, Evidence Contracts, Provenance Contracts, Storage, Identity, Promotion Contracts.

## Consumers

Jarvis, Foundry, MICE, internal applications, and domains.
