# MIDAS

## Purpose

Owns measurement and insight sidecars.

## Owns

Measurement frames, derived metrics, indicators, insight sidecars, and measurement evidence.

## Does Not Own

Telemetry infrastructure, policy decisions, reality preservation, or value judgment.

## Inputs

Observations, evidence, relationships, assets, and CivPhys-derived mechanics.

## Outputs

Measurements, indicators, sidecars, and measurement-backed evidence.

## Dependencies

ARK, Evidence Contracts, CivPhys, WEAVE.

## Consumers

Reasoning, VALOR, Jarvis, Views, and domains.
