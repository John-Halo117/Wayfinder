rather than filling gaps by inference.

   `WAYFINDER.md`?

   documents, or is there a separate amendment ledger that should be inherited
   by Foundry phases?

   future amendment process?
4. What is the explicit promotion path from ADR to constitutional amendment?
5. Which capability registry entries are canonical today versus early
   placeholders?
6. What is the formal boundary between Canonical Language as derived substrate
   and future knowledge governance contracts?
7. Which operations signals are required before a capability may be called
   Operational in the maturity framework?
8. How should generated knowledge artifacts be referenced by Foundry without
   treating generated representations as constitutional sources?
9. What is the canonical policy for retiring or superseding deprecated aliases
   once compatibility windows close?
