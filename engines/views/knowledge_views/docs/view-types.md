# View Types

## Timeline

Chronological projection of promoted knowledge.

## Architecture

Groups promoted knowledge into engines, services, contracts, capabilities, and
architecture references.

## Conversation

Reconstructs conversation-linked promoted knowledge from source observation
references. It does not summarize.

## Concept

Shows concept-related promoted knowledge and provenance.

## Decision

Shows decision-related promoted knowledge, review history, and promotion history.

## Glossary

Projects glossary-like promoted knowledge, aliases, deprecated terms, owners,
definitions, and acronyms.

## Capsule

Projects approved capsule and continuity-related knowledge.

## Objective

Projects objectives, TODOs, progress, dependencies, and related decisions.

## Review Queue

Projects candidate records for human review without promoting them.

## Search Results

Formats retrieval responses with ranking explanations and provenance.

## Composed

Combines existing view results without duplicating knowledge.
