# Roads Domain

Owns roads, driveways, paths, gates, bridges, parking, loading, turning radii,
snow or debris management, and emergency vehicle access.

Canonical here:

- vehicle and pedestrian circulation
- surface assemblies
- drainage interfaces
- gates and barriers
- load limits
- maintenance requirements

## Purpose

Define access as physical infrastructure for people, emergency response,
delivery, equipment, maintenance, and future construction.

## Lifecycle

Road patterns are reusable. Actual access routes are verified through surveys,
construction records, inspections, and maintenance records.

## Generation Targets

Access plans, emergency route maps, surface maintenance schedules, and drainage
coordination diagrams.
