# Road And Access Pattern

Roads and access routes are service infrastructure, not decorative circulation.

## Responsibilities

- support emergency response
- support daily access
- support construction and delivery
- support maintenance equipment
- avoid damaging drainage behavior
- preserve future expansion access

## Required Capabilities

- load class
- surface class
- turning and passing constraints
- drainage interface
- snow, mud, debris, or erosion strategy where relevant
- gate or barrier interfaces
- inspection points
- maintenance procedure

## Expansion Rules

Road placement should avoid blocking future utility corridors, building pads,
water paths, agricultural expansion, and equipment movement.
