# Greywater Utility Standard

Greywater systems route lightly used water for reuse or disposal under explicit
health, access, and failure constraints.

## Required Standard

- identify source fixtures
- identify allowed and prohibited inputs
- identify diverter, filter, storage, distribution, and disposal path
- document health constraints and local compliance requirements
- provide cleaning and bypass access

## Expansion Rule

Greywater reuse should be designed as an optional interface that can be
bypassed or retired without disabling the source fixture.
