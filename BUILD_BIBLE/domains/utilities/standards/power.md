# Power Utility Standard

Power systems provide energy safely, visibly, and with reserve capacity.

## Required Standard

- identify source, storage, distribution, protection, grounding, and isolation
- publish capacity at service, panel, circuit, receptacle, equipment, and spare
  interface levels
- preserve access to panels, disconnects, junctions, and major raceways
- label circuits and loads
- reserve physical and electrical capacity for future generation, storage,
  vehicle charging, tools, sensors, and unknown loads

## Failure Isolation

Power failures should isolate by source, panel, circuit, equipment, or zone
without disabling unrelated critical capabilities when practical.
