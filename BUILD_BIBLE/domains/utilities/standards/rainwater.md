# Rainwater Utility Standard

Rainwater systems collect, move, store, overflow, infiltrate, or reuse roof and
site water.

## Required Standard

- identify catchment surface
- identify first flush, conveyance, storage, treatment, overflow, and discharge
- publish capacity and overflow behavior
- preserve cleanout and inspection access
- prevent hidden foundation, road, and erosion impacts

## Expansion Rule

Rainwater systems should reserve interfaces for storage, irrigation,
greenhouse, livestock, fire, and emergency non-potable uses where appropriate.
