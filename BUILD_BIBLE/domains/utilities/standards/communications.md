# Communications Utility Standard

Communications systems provide physical pathways for data, signaling, voice,
control, and future protocols.

## Required Standard

- identify service entrance, rack or distribution point, cabling routes,
  terminations, wireless coverage intent, grounding coordination, and spare
  pathways
- label cables and ports
- preserve pull paths and bend constraints
- separate communications from incompatible services

## Expansion Rule

Communications should favor conduit, accessible cable trays, pull strings,
service loops, and spare ports over sealed-in single-purpose wiring.
