# Water Utility Standard

Water systems provide potable or non-potable water through explicit,
serviceable, and isolatable interfaces.

## Required Standard

- identify source, treatment, storage, pressure, distribution, isolation, and
  monitoring
- label valves and branches
- publish fixture and equipment interfaces
- preserve freeze, leak, contamination, pressure, and backflow constraints
- provide service access to filters, pumps, tanks, valves, and manifolds

## Reserve Capacity

Water systems should reserve route, pressure, flow, storage, and treatment
capacity for future fixtures, irrigation, livestock, greenhouse, fire, and
emergency uses.
