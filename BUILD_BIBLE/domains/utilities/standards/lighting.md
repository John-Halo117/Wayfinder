# Lighting Utility Standard

Lighting systems provide visual capability, safety, task support, security, and
wayfinding.

## Required Standard

- identify lighting zones
- identify fixture interfaces
- identify control interfaces
- document service access
- preserve replacement compatibility
- separate life-safety lighting constraints from comfort or scene lighting

## Expansion Rule

Lighting should preserve low-voltage, sensor, daylight, automation, and manual
fallback interfaces where practical.
