# Maintenance Domain

Owns cross-domain maintenance strategy, recurring service programs, inspection
cadence, spare parts strategy, and reliability obligations.

Canonical here:

- maintenance doctrine
- recurring schedules
- service access standards
- spare part classes
- maintenance-generated observations

Specific procedures may live with the owning scope when they are unique to
that scope.

## Purpose

Define reusable maintenance procedures that preserve physical reality,
serviceability, history, and digital twin accuracy.

## Lifecycle

Maintenance standards are reusable. Actual work is recorded in lifecycle
service records and linked back to affected physical scopes.

## Generation Targets

Inspection checklists, maintenance schedules, service dashboards, replacement
forecasts, and digital twin sync reports.
