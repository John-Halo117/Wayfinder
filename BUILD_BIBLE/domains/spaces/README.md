# Spaces Domain

Owns rooms, zones, closets, cabinets, service cavities, mechanical rooms, and
other bounded interior or exterior spaces.

Canonical here:

- room spines
- cabinet spines
- spatial boundaries
- access and service clearances
- local capability publication

## Purpose

Define reusable spatial standards so every room, cabinet, service cavity, and
mechanical area publishes capabilities, interfaces, constraints, service paths,
and digital twin state.

## Lifecycle

Spatial patterns are reusable. Actual rooms and cabinets are physical scope
records verified by observations and service records.

## Generation Targets

Room data sheets, service maps, lighting plans, plumbing diagrams, maintenance
checklists, and digital twin exports.
