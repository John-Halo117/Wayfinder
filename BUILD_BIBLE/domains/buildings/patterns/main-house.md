# Main House Pattern

The main house is the primary residential platform for shelter, daily life,
utilities, safety, storage, serviceability, and long-term adaptation.

## Required Capabilities

- durable envelope
- code-compliant egress
- primary building spine
- kitchen, sanitation, sleeping, storage, and living support
- water, waste, power, ventilation, lighting, communications, security, and
  monitoring interfaces where available
- maintainable mechanical core
- accessible service documentation
- future expansion interfaces

## Constraints

The main house must prioritize safety, indoor environmental quality, repairable
assemblies, and non-destructive service access.

## Serviceability

Critical systems should concentrate around mechanical rooms, utility walls,
chases, service corridors, accessible attics, accessible crawl spaces, or
removable assemblies.

## Expansion

Expansion should first consume reserved panel capacity, spare conduits,
accessible chases, utility walls, and planned addition interfaces.
