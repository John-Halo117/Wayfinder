# Greenhouse Pattern

The greenhouse is a controlled growing environment and wet utility structure.

## Required Capabilities

- solar exposure management
- ventilation
- water distribution
- drainage
- humidity-tolerant materials
- environmental monitoring
- pest and disease inspection access
- serviceable growing infrastructure

## Optional Capabilities

- thermal mass
- supplemental heat
- shade control
- misting
- fertigation
- propagation benches
- rainwater interface
- automation interface

## Constraints

Greenhouses are wet areas. Water, condensation, corrosion, biological growth,
and freeze risk must be designed as normal conditions, not exceptions.

## Serviceability

Plumbing, drains, sensors, fans, vents, screens, and glazing should be
serviceable without dismantling growing systems.

## Expansion

Reserve mounting, water, power, sensor, ventilation, and data interfaces for
future climate control and growing systems.
