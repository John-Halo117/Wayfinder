# Land Domain

Owns parcels, terrain, soil, geology, vegetation baseline, natural water,
viewsheds, microclimates, easements, setbacks, and permanent land constraints.

Canonical here:

- parcel and boundary facts after verification
- soil and terrain records
- easements and land-use constraints
- natural drainage and hydrology context

Generated survey drawings belong in `generation/diagrams/` or
`generation/cad/`.
