# Stable Principle Promotion

The Build Bible promotes only stable, repeatedly established physical design
principles into canonical doctrine.

## Rule

Promote a principle once, at the highest accurate abstraction. Lower-level
documents inherit or reference it instead of duplicating it.

## Promotion Criteria

- repeated across multiple design contexts
- durable over time
- independent of a single product or implementation
- useful for future generation or review
- traceable to doctrine, contract, pattern, standard, or lifecycle effect

## Non-Promotion Criteria

- temporary preference
- property-specific implementation idea
- unverified site fact
- product selection
- one-off design detail
- generated output

## Compression Rule

When two principles explain the same phenomenon, unify them into the smaller
enduring abstraction.
