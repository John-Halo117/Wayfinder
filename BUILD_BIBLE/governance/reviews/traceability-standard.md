# Traceability Standard

Major patterns must expose their upstream doctrine and downstream generated
uses without duplicating canonical facts.

## Required Cross-References

- applicable doctrine
- applicable contracts
- applicable schemas
- Engineering Decision Records
- related metrics
- anti-patterns
- reliability records
- verification standards
- generated artifacts
- future reality records that will instantiate the pattern

## Rule

Traceability is navigation, not duplication. A document should link to the
canonical owner of each concept.
