# Acceptance And Verification Standard

Acceptance verifies that a physical scope is ready for its intended lifecycle
phase or operational state.

## Required Acceptance Fields

- accepted scope ID
- acceptance phase
- test method
- evidence required
- pass criteria
- fail criteria
- verifier
- date
- open issues
- digital twin update

## Common Acceptance Tests

- leak tested
- air sealed
- electrical tested
- network certified
- water pressure tested
- thermal scanned
- documentation complete
- digital twin updated

## Rule

Commissioning is incomplete until acceptance evidence and digital twin state
are updated.
