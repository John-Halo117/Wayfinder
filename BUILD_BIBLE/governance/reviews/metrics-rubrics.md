# Metrics Rubrics

Metrics define what good means. P2.1 metrics are qualitative rubrics, not
numeric scoring schemas.

## Rubric Bands

- `unknown`: not evaluated.
- `poor`: hidden dependencies, unclear access, or high reconstruction burden.
- `adequate`: usable but constrained, with known tradeoffs.
- `good`: serviceable, documented, and expandable within published limits.
- `excellent`: observable, modular, resilient, replaceable, and evolution-ready.

## Canonical Metrics

- serviceability index
- evolvability score
- observability coverage
- reserve capacity
- passive resilience
- maintenance burden
- modularity
- accessibility
- replaceability
- water independence
- energy independence

## Rule

Metrics guide review and comparison. They do not replace evidence, engineering
judgment, code requirements, or acceptance testing.
