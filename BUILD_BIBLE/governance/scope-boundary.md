# Scope Boundary

The Build Bible governs physical reality only.

## In Scope

- Land, parcels, easements, terrain, soil, water, drainage, roads, paths, and
  exterior infrastructure.
- Buildings, rooms, envelopes, assemblies, finishes, fixtures, furniture, and
  storage.
- Electrical, water, waste, HVAC, lighting, low-voltage, networking, security,
  monitoring, automation hardware, and service infrastructure.
- Gardens, orchards, food forests, greenhouses, livestock infrastructure, and
  agricultural systems.
- Equipment, tools, vehicles, materials, spares, construction methods,
  inspection records, maintenance records, and future expansion capacity.

## Out of Scope

- Wayfinder software internals.
- Application source code.
- Purely digital workflows that do not represent or control physical systems.
- Personal notes that do not alter physical intent, verified reality, or
  lifecycle obligations.

## Boundary Rule

If a software artifact directly represents, controls, monitors, or generates a
physical system, the physical intent belongs here and the software
implementation belongs outside this tree.
