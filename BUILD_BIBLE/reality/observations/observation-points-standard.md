# Observation Points Standard

Observation points are planned places where reality is repeatedly checked.

## Required Observation Classes

- water movement
- utility ingress
- structural movement
- weather and microclimate
- security perimeter
- access and road condition
- soil and vegetation
- equipment condition
- storage condition
- indoor environmental quality

## Required Fields

- observation point ID
- observed scope IDs
- location
- purpose
- method
- frequency
- evidence type
- trigger for escalation
- linked canonical records

## Rule

Observation points should be easy to revisit, easy to photograph or measure,
and stable enough that readings can be compared over decades.
