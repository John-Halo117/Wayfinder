# Fractal Spines

Every scope owns a spine appropriate to its scale.

## Spine Definition

A spine is the organized path through which a scope distributes services,
interfaces, monitoring, reserve capacity, and service access.

## Recursive Examples

- Property spine distributes site utilities and access.
- Building spine distributes primary building services.
- Room spine distributes local services and controls.
- Cabinet spine distributes local power, lighting, data, and service access.
- Appliance spine exposes replaceable interfaces and maintenance access.

## Distributed Capabilities

Each spine may distribute:

- power
- water
- drainage
- data
- ventilation
- monitoring
- expansion capacity
- inspection access
- service access

## Rule

The same questions apply at every scale: what enters, what leaves, what is
reserved, what can fail, what can be inspected, and what can be replaced without
destructive access.
