# Optionality

The Build Bible does not predict future technology. It preserves the ability to
adopt future technology.

## Unknown Future Systems

Design for unknown sensing, robotics, drones, LiDAR, networking, energy
systems, water systems, and automation by preserving:

- accessible routes
- spare power
- spare data paths
- mounting points
- sight lines
- structural attachment points
- exterior and interior service zones
- known constraints

## Publication Rule

Every location should publish what can be added there and what would block
addition.
