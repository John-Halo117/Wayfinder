# Platform, Not Product

The house and property are long-lived platforms, not finished products.

## Living Architecture

A homestead is never complete. It is continuously observed, maintained,
improved, and adapted. Success is measured not by reaching a final state, but
by preserving coherence, capability, and stewardship through decades of change.

## Rule

Prefer expansion over remodeling. Prefer accessible infrastructure over buried
infrastructure. Prefer reversible assemblies over destructive dependencies.

## Design Consequence

Any scope that consumes infrastructure should publish:

- current capability
- spare capacity
- service access
- upgrade path
- replacement path
- future connection points
