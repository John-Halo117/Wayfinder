# Reliability

Reliability is the long-term ability of a physical capability to continue
providing intended service through stress, degradation, maintenance, and
failure.

## Purpose

Make expected degradation, failure, detection, isolation, repair, recovery,
monitoring, inspection, and resilience explicit before a system is built.

## Lifecycle

Reliability records evolve through observations, inspections, maintenance,
service records, and Engineering Decision Records.

## Generation Targets

Reliability reviews, failure-impact maps, maintenance forecasts, monitoring
plans, recovery checklists, and resilience reports.
