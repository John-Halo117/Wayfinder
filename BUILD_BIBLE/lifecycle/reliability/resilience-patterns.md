# Resilience Patterns

Resilience patterns preserve capability during stress, degradation, outage, or
failure.

## Patterns

- graceful degradation
- manual fallback
- passive operation
- local-first operation
- isolation by zone
- redundant route
- spare capacity
- modular replacement
- bypass path
- visible failure
- safe failure

## Rule

Resilience should be designed into interfaces, spines, access paths, and
operational states rather than patched on after failure.
