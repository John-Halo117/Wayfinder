# Verification Standards

Verification standards define how a claim about condition, capacity, function,
or completion becomes accepted.

## Required Fields

- verified scope or capability class
- claim being verified
- method
- required evidence
- acceptance criteria
- rejection criteria
- verifier role
- verification date
- uncertainty
- digital twin update

## Acceptance Examples

- leak tested
- air sealed
- electrical tested
- network certified
- water pressure tested
- thermal scanned
- documentation complete
- digital twin updated
