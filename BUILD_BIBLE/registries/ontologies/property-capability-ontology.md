# Property Capability Ontology

The property capability ontology classifies what a physical property can
provide without describing any specific property.

## Capability Domains

- structures
- infrastructure
- land
- water
- energy
- communications
- agriculture
- transportation
- storage
- security
- environmental systems
- workshops
- equipment

## Rule

Capabilities are stable; products and implementations are replaceable. Domain
records should describe capabilities first and products second.
