# Constitutional Laws

## Reality First

Reality precedes representation. Observation precedes interpretation. Evidence
precedes conclusions. Reality is append-only.

## Law of Theseus

Capabilities, continuity, and objectives are preserved while implementations
may be replaced.

## One Concept, One Home

Every concept has one canonical owner. Other locations reference that owner.

## Closed Under Extension

Prefer extension and composition over modification.

## Capability First

Capabilities are stable. Implementations evolve.

## Service First

Shared infrastructure belongs in services. Unique behavior belongs in engines.

## Ephemeral First

Working state is disposable unless intentionally promoted.

## Proof Before Promotion

Durable knowledge requires an ephemeral proof.
