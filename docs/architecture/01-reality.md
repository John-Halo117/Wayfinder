# 01 Reality

Reality is the external world and source material before representation,
interpretation, knowledge, or action. Wayfinder's first law is that reality
precedes representation.

## Owns

- Source existence.
- Append-only evidence posture.
- The principle that interpretation must not overwrite source truth.

## Does Not Own

- Parsed observations.
- Derived representations.
- Knowledge, search indexes, summaries, embeddings, or views.

## Repository Evidence

- `WAYFINDER.md`
- `constitution/laws.md`
- `constitution/architecture.md`
- `docs/system-architecture-v1.md`

## Health

Reality health is indirect: every downstream artifact must preserve provenance
to reality or source evidence. Current generated Knowledge reports
`provenance_missing_count = 0`.

## Missing Pieces

- A future action-reobservation workflow is not implemented.
- External reality sources beyond ChatGPT export remain unimplemented or
  preserved in legacy.
