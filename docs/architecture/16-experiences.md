# 16 Experiences

Experiences are presentation surfaces and composed interactions. They sit
after navigation and before actions.

## Owns

- Human-facing composition.
- Dashboards, reports, voice, maps, mobile, APIs, daily brief, playlists, and
  mixed-media surfaces.
- Presentation-only state.

## Does Not Own

- Reality preservation.
- Reasoning.
- Navigation decisions.
- Action authority.
