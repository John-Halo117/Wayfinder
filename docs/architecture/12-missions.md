# 12 Missions

Missions organize objectives, constraints, success criteria, and horizons.
Missions must precede navigation.

## Owns

- Objective framing.
- Success criteria.
- Constraints and horizons.
- Mission progress surfaces.

## Does Not Own

- Route selection.
- Action execution.
- Reality preservation.

## Mission Health

Observed concepts:

- Objective View in Knowledge Views.
- Mission / Objective language in Canon and generated Knowledge.
- MICE owns commitments and accountability, not mission framing itself.

Gaps:

- Mission, Objective, Mission Band, and Horizon need clearer canonical
  ownership.
- Success criteria exist in prompts/docs but not in a reusable mission schema.

Recommendation:

Define mission contracts before Jarvis navigation work expands.
