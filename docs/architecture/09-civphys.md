# 09 CivPhys

CivPhys is Wayfinder's universal mechanics layer: pressure, flow, friction,
potential, membranes, trust, slack, and related primitives.

## Owns

- Mechanics vocabulary.
- Primitive roots for derived mechanics.
- Cross-domain physical/social/technical dynamics language.

## Does Not Own

- Runtime execution.
- View presentation.
- Domain-specific scoring implementations unless promoted separately.

## Canonical Owner

- `constitution/civphys.md`

## Health

CivPhys is well documented constitutionally but lightly connected to active
runtime proofs.

## Migration Path

Use CivPhys primitives as reasoning inputs later. Avoid premature service or
engine extraction until at least one reasoning or measurement proof needs them.
