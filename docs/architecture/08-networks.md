# 08 Networks

Networks are relationship structures among assets, observations, concepts,
systems, people, providers, and contexts.

## Owns

- Relationship topology once WEAVE exists.
- Network surfaces for later reasoning and navigation.
- Dependency, capability, media, and mission graph projections.

## Does Not Own

- Explicit source relationships before ARK preservation.
- Storage implementation.
- Final reasoning or navigation.

## Current State

- ARK preserves Source Relationships.
- `Knowledge/Graph/` contains generated derived graph artifacts.
- WEAVE is defined but not implemented.

## Health

- Generated compile report: circular references `0`.
- Source Relationships remain distinct from topology in ADR-0002.

## Gaps

- WEAVE needs a Stage 1/2 contract and proof after ARK and Storage stabilize.
- Media Graph is planned but not implemented.
